About Us

The Institute for Professional Development (IPD) is a private institution established to bridge the gap between formal and informal education. Our mission is to deliver professional training programs that empower individuals and create opportunities for self-employment, career advancement, and community development.

Our Vision

To become the leading multi-professional training center transforming lives through high-quality, accessible education in the Eastern Corridor of Ghana.

Our Objectives

  • Increase professional careers and employment opportunities in the region
  • Make quality education accessible to everyone, regardless of background
  • Prepare students for future success with practical, career-focused training

Our Values

  • Excellence in education
  • Commitment to community growth
  • Accessibility and affordability

Accreditations

  • Commission for Technical and Vocational Training
  • National Technical and Vocational Institute
